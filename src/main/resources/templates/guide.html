COVID-19 verification guide

How to scan the QR-CODE on your vaccination card
The QR-CODE is located on your COVID-19 vaccination card typically at the bottom right on the cover page.
You can scan the code by using the camera of your mobile device/tablet. For devices that do not support scanning QR code directly from the device camera. You can download a QR-CODE scanner application on the Apple store or google playstore.
Note: if you are unable to scan your QR-CODE due to physical damage please visit the vaccination site where you took the 1st dose or any of the supersites located in all the 36 states and FCT for replacement. The list can be accessed on NPHCDA website: www.nphcda.gov.ng
Incorrect details? Name, DOB, ID Type and ID Number
a. Click on the “update” button at the top of your e-certificate.
Note: if you do not see this button at the top of your e-certificate. please contact the NPHCDA COVID-19 call center on 0700 220 1122 for assistance
b. When you click on “update” button, a popup will appear for you to enter the phone number or email address [1] you provided during registration.
Click the “confirm” button when you have entered your phone number or email [2]
c. if you enter the correct details. you would be granted access and redirected to your profile page where you will be able to edit any incorrect detail.
click ‘save profile’ [1] when you are done
Incorrect vaccination details? status, batch number ...
a. Click on the “update” button at the top of your e-certificate.
Note: if you do not see this button at the top of your e-certificate.
please call the NPHCDA COVID-19 call centre on 0700 220 1122 for assistance.
b. When you click on “update” button, a popup will appear for you to to enter the phone number or email address [1] you provided during registration.
Click the “confirm” button [2]
c. if you enter the correct details. you will be granted access and redirected to your profile page.
d. To view your vaccination record, click on ‘vaccination history’ [1]
e. Please contact any of the numbers provided at the bottom of the page to assist in resolving any issue on the vaccination history.
If you are unable to contact them, please call the NPHCDA COVID-19 call center on 0700 220 1122
Due for second dose?
A notification will be displayed as soon as you scan your QR code.
A link to the list of the vaccination sites will be displayed to enable you select your preferred site.
ERROR: record has not been linked or might not exist message
After scanning your QR code, if a prompt showing “record has not been linked or might not exist” pops up,
please call the NPHCDA's COVID-19 call centre on 0700 220 1122 or the state representatives as listed on the portal for assistance.
